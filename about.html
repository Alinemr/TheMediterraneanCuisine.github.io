The history of Mediterranean Cuisine dates back to 9 BC.( Essid, M.Y.2012, p 57) and some vital aspects have influenced the development of Mediterranean diet throughout history.
I agree with (Essid, M.Y. 2012, p 52) who stated that several constraints should be considered in order to comprehend the history of Mediterranean Cuisine,
for example political situations, natural disasters, cultural interactions, and also change in life styles.


Mediterranean cuisine comprises of multiple constituents, particularly fruits and vegetables, olive oil, dairy, and meat products.
Consequently, Mediterranean cuisine, through various findings, has been proven to be beneficial in terms of lowering the risk of various diseases.
Moreover, (Altomare et al 2013, 449) suggested that the Mediterranean cuisine, however rudimentary in terms of its primary ingredients,
offers a multitude of dishes and tastes that have been utilised for many centuries. Furthermore, (Altomare et al 2013, 449 ) proposed that the cuisine is
one that has conformed to various ethnicities that pertain to the Mediterranean Basin.
